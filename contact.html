Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our process and pricing.

What is your typical turnaround time? expand_more
Most standard websites take 4-8 weeks from start to launch. More complex applications or large-scale e-commerce sites can take 3-6 months depending on requirements.
Do you offer ongoing support? expand_more
Yes! We offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. We can also provide ad-hoc support for future updates.
How does the payment process work? expand_more
Typically, we require a 50% deposit to secure your slot in our schedule and begin the discovery phase. The remaining 50% is due upon project completion and sign-off, prior to launch.
